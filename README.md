## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Discereon Radio free to use?
Yes, 100% free. No subscription, no account, no payment needed. Open the website and start listening.

### Do I need to create an account?
No account required. Just visit the site and click on any station to start listening immediately.

### Can I listen on my phone?
Yes. The website works on all devices — smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktops. Any device with a modern browser and internet connection can stream radio through Discereon.

### Do I need to download an app?
No app download needed. Everything works directly in your browser.

### What formats are the streams in?
Most stations stream in **MP3** or **AAC+** format. The station listing shows the format and bitrate so you can pick the quality you want. Higher bitrate (like 256 kbps or 320 kbps) means better audio quality.

### What's the difference between FM, AM, and internet radio?
**FM radio** is your standard local broadcast radio (good for music). **AM radio** is longer-range broadcast (often used for news and talk shows). **Internet radio** is streamed purely online and can come from anywhere in the world — many internet-only stations don't have a traditional broadcast signal at all. Discereon Radio includes all three types.

### Can I listen to radio stations from my home country while abroad?
Yes! That's one of the best things about online radio. Whether you're traveling, living abroad, or just curious about music from another country, you can tune in from anywhere in the world.

### Why does online radio sound better than regular FM sometimes?
Many online radio streams broadcast at high bitrates (128 kbps to 320 kbps) which can actually sound cleaner than over-the-air FM, especially if you're far from a transmitter or in a building that blocks signals.

### Can I use it as background music while working?
Absolutely. Many people use online radio for exactly this — jazz stations, lo-fi stations, classical music, or ambient/chill stations are perfect for focus and background listening.

### Is there a way to find stations by music genre?
Yes. Discereon Radio lets you browse stations by country, and many country pages include station details like genre, language, and format so you can pick the right one for your mood.

## 💡 Tips for the Best Online Radio Experience

- **Pick a higher bitrate station** for better sound — look for 128 kbps or higher
- **AAC+ streams** often sound better than MP3 at the same bitrate
- **Use wired headphones or a Bluetooth speaker** for the best audio experience
- **Cast to your smart speaker** — Chromecast and AirPlay let you send the stream from your phone to a speaker
- **Bookmark your favorite stations** so you can come back to them quickly
- **Try stations from countries you've never heard radio from before** — it's one of the most underrated ways to discover new music and culture
